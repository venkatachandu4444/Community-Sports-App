Account Deletion Instructions

We're sorry to see you go! If you've decided to delete your Community Sports App account, this page will guide you through the process.

Important - Account Deletion is Permanent

Before proceeding, please understand that deleting your account is an irreversible action. Once deleted:

  • Your account cannot be recovered or reactivated
  • All your personal information will be permanently deleted
  • Your profile, photos, and uploaded content will be removed
  • Your match statistics and event history will be lost
  • You will lose access to all teams and communities you've joined

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, bio, sports preferences, skill level
  • User Content: Photos, videos, highlights you've uploaded
  • Activity Data: Match scores, team memberships, event participations
  • Communication History: Notifications, messages, and app preferences

What Happens to Your Contributions?

Some data may be retained in anonymized form to maintain the integrity of the platform:

  • Match scores and statistics you contributed may remain visible but will be anonymized
  • Event details for matches you organized may remain for historical records
  • Public comments or posts may be retained in anonymized form

Note: These anonymized data cannot be traced back to you or your deleted account.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete via Mobile App (Recommended)

1

Open the App

Launch the Community Sports App on your mobile device and ensure you're logged in to the account you wish to delete.

2

Navigate to Settings

Tap on your profile picture or navigate to the main menu, then select "Settings" or "Account Settings".

3

Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" or "Account Management" section. This is typically located at the bottom of the settings page.

4

Confirm Deletion

Read the warning message carefully, then tap "Delete My Account" or "Confirm Deletion". You may be asked to enter your password or verify your identity.

5

Verify via Email

Check your registered email for a verification message. Click the confirmation link to complete the deletion process.

6

Account Deleted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You'll receive a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you're unable to access the app or prefer to delete your account via email, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@communitysportsapp.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address and username in the email body
  4. Provide a brief reason for deletion (optional)
  5. Our support team will verify your identity and process the deletion within 5-7 business days
  6. You'll receive a confirmation email once your account is deleted

Alternatives to Account Deletion

If you're considering deleting your account due to privacy concerns or temporary reasons, consider these alternatives:

  • Deactivate Temporarily: Temporarily disable your account instead of permanently deleting it (if available)
  • Adjust Privacy Settings: Update your privacy settings to control what information is shared
  • Remove Content: Delete specific photos, videos, or posts without deleting your entire account
  • Take a Break: Simply log out and take a break without deleting your account
  • Update Preferences: Adjust notification settings or content preferences

Data Retention Timeline

Here's what happens to your data after you request account deletion:

  • Immediately: Your account is marked for deletion and you lose access to the app
  • Within 7 days: Your profile becomes invisible to other users
  • Within 30 days: All personal information and user content is permanently deleted from our systems
  • After 30 days: Only anonymized statistical data may remain for platform analytics

Need Help?

If you're experiencing issues with the app or have concerns, our support team is here to help before you decide to delete your account. We may be able to resolve your concerns!

Contact Support

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us:

Email: support@communitysportsapp.com

Subject Line: Account Deletion Inquiry

Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours

After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will receive a final confirmation email
  • You can create a new account in the future using the same email address
  • Previous data from your deleted account will not be restored
  • You'll need to start fresh with a new profile
